About Us

We are a team of passionate developers building the future of operating systems.

Our Mission

To create an operating system that is not only powerful and secure but also beautiful and a joy to use. We believe that the user interface is just as important as the kernel.

The Technology

ModernOS is built on a custom kernel that provides a stable foundation for our unique Flutter-based userspace. By running Flutter directly on the metal, we achieve unparalleled performance and fluidity.
